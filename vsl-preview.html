Phase 1

VSL Preview Script: Anjie & RJ Muhammad

Format: Short preview (2-3 minutes). Not a full sales VSL. Designed to humanise the offer and reduce friction at the application stage.

VSL Context

This is a preview-length script for the "A Word From Anjie and RJ" section of the landing page. It is not meant to close. It is meant to do the following three things:

  1. Show that this is a real couple talking to real couples, not a polished guru production
  2. Name the exact pain point the mentorship solves (accountability as a couple, not information)
  3. Route warm viewers to the application without overselling

Script: "A Word From Anjie and RJ" (2:30 approx.)

[Open: Anjie and RJ together on camera. Home environment preferred. Not a studio. Casual, warm.]

RJ: Hey, if you are watching this, you have probably already spent some time inside the Rich by Intention world. Maybe you found us through the podcast, maybe through the book, maybe a friend sent you an episode at the exact right moment.

Anjie: And if you have been following for a while, you already know our story. Six figures of debt. Two people in a marriage with genuinely different relationships with money. And a decision to do it publicly, because we felt like too many couples were handling the money conversation alone, in private, and feeling ashamed about it.

RJ: What we learned doing this publicly is that the information is not the hard part. Most couples who are serious about paying off debt know what to do. The hard part is doing it together, consistently, when life does not cooperate with the spreadsheet.

Anjie: That is exactly why we built the Couples Wealth Mentorship. Not because couples need more content. They need someone in the room with them.

RJ: Twelve weeks. Live weekly calls with both of us. An accountability pod with one other couple at roughly the same point in their journey. Monthly office hours if you want to talk through something specific to your household.

Anjie: This is not a course you buy and finish someday. It is a container where both of you are doing the work, at the same time, with real accountability built in.

RJ: If you are ready to stop carrying this alone, the application is right below this video. It takes four minutes. We review every application ourselves and we will be back to you within 48 hours.

Anjie: We are looking forward to meeting you both.

[End. Cut to application CTA.]

Production Notes

  • Film together, not separately. This is a couples offer. The video must show the couple.
  • Casual environment (kitchen table, living room). Do not over-produce. The warmth of the home environment matches the peer-to-peer register of the brand.
  • No teleprompter if possible. Conversational delivery over polished read-through.
  • Length target: 2:00-2:45. Any longer risks losing the mobile viewer before the CTA.
  • Swap in real video on Day 1 of install. Landing page ships with a static poster placeholder that visually reads as "watch the intro" until the real VSL is filmed.
  • Suggested thumbnail: Anjie and RJ mid-conversation, natural expression, text overlay: "A Word From Anjie and RJ - Rich by Intention"
