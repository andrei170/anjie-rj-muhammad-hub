Architecture

Funnel Blueprint: Anjie & RJ Muhammad

Current Funnel State (Day 0)

Podcast (award-winning)  -->  Listener warms  -->  No conversion point
IG @richbyintention      -->  Follower engages  -->  Bio link to website
richbyintention.com      -->  Visitor lands     -->  Course purchase or exit
Book (Amazon)            -->  Reader finishes   -->  No follow-up routing

Gap: No application flow, no email capture at podcast level, no live mentorship tier, no alumni path.

Proposed Funnel Architecture (Post-Install)

TRAFFIC SOURCES
  Podcast episode outro  -->  richbyintention.com/mentorship
  IG bio link            -->  Calculator quiz (richbyintention.com/quiz)
  Book back-page QR      -->  Calculator quiz
  Existing email list    -->  Broadcast announcement of cohort

LEAD CAPTURE
  Calculator quiz ("Couples Debt Payoff Timeline")
    |-- Completion --> email captured
    |-- Result email: personalised debt-free date estimate
    |-- Nurture sequence begins (8-touch)

EMAIL NURTURE (8-touch, 14-day sequence)
  Touch 1: Quiz result + story
  Touch 2: Bottleneck naming (accountability gap)
  Touch 3: Social proof (cohort graduate story)
  Touch 4: What the mentorship actually includes
  Touch 5: FAQ (faith, pricing, time commitment)
  Touch 6: Application opens
  Touch 7: Application deadline reminder
  Touch 8: Final call (cohort cap/closing soon)

APPLICATION & ENROLLMENT
  Typeform application (10 questions, couples fill together)
    |-- Submit --> booking confirmation email
    |-- Calendly: "Cohort Fit Call" (15 min, no pitch, just logistics)
    |-- Fit call --> enrollment link sent
    |-- Payment: $2K-$4K per couple (one-time or 2-payment split)

COHORT DELIVERY (12 weeks)
  Week 1-2: Foundation (shared financial values, current numbers mapped)
  Week 3-5: System install (debt payoff framework, budget structure)
  Week 6-8: Momentum (accountability pods, weekly check-ins)
  Week 9-11: Obstacles (marriage-money friction, income variance)
  Week 12: Celebration + Alumni Circle onboarding

POST-COHORT
  Alumni Circle ($97/mo or $797/yr)
    |-- Monthly group call with Anjie and RJ
    |-- Private community (Skool)
    |-- Accountability pods continue
    |-- First access to future offers and cohorts

Phase Map

Phase 1 - Core Funnel Install (Days 1-7)

  • Landing page live (richbyintention.com/mentorship or GitHub Pages)
  • Calculator quiz live (richbyintention.com/quiz)
  • Application form configured (Typeform)
  • Cohort booking flow active (Calendly)
  • Payment page live (Whop or Stripe)
  • Email broadcast to existing list announcing cohort

Phase 2 - Nurture Layer (Days 4-10)

  • 8-touch email nurture sequence written and scheduled
  • Podcast outro language drafted and recorded by Anjie/RJ
  • Episode description CTAs updated across back-catalog (top 20 episodes)
  • Book back-page QR code routed to quiz

Phase 3 - Content Compound (Post-install, ongoing)

  • Weekly short-form content calendar running (Pillar 1-4 from content strategy)
  • Cohort behind-the-scenes content begins with cohort 1
  • Alumni Circle structure built and onboarded

Phase 4 - Scale (Post-cohort 1)

  • Cohort 1 data used to refine sales page and application
  • Alumni Circle launching with first cohort graduates
  • Retargeting infrastructure added (pixel, email segmentation by engagement)
  • Referral mechanic added (alumni refer couples at discount or commission)
