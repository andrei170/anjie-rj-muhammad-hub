01

No Couples Mentorship Cohort: The Award-Winning Podcast Feeds Goodwill, Not Revenue

26K audience. Award-winning podcast. Book. Self-paced courses. No live, accountable container where Anjie and RJ are in the room with couples who want more than earbuds. The self-paced courses teach the system. The cohort installs it with accountability. Every listener who finishes an episode and wants hands-on help has nowhere to go except a self-paced product that does not solve their core problem: staying aligned as a couple through the payoff.

Cost of Waiting

$40-160K per cohort in mentorship revenue not collected. At 20-40 couples per quarterly cohort at $2K-$4K per couple, Year-1 floor is $160-640K in unbooked revenue while the podcast continues producing warm, qualified leads that walk back out the door with no conversion path.

6 months from now ·Two cohort cycles completed by a competitor who serves the same couples-wealth niche. Your warm podcast audience has had 25+ more episodes and no offer to step into. The relationship deepens; the revenue does not.

Fix · Couples Wealth Mentorship cohort. $2K-$4K per couple. 12 weeks, live weekly calls, co-couple accountability pods. Quarterly cadence. Year-1 floor: $40-160K per cohort, $160-640K annually. Existing courses stay live as the entry tier.
Fast Install
02

No Email Capture at Podcast Level: Warm Leads Walk Off Every Week

Every episode releases a warm, self-qualified listener back to Spotify or Apple with no route to an email list. There is no lead magnet linked in episode descriptions. No "go to richbyintention.com/quiz" baked into the episode outro. No Couples Debt Payoff Timeline Calculator that captures their contact information before they leave the ecosystem. Meanwhile every episode is a micro-sales event where the couple self-selects ("this is us") - and then disappears.

Cost of Waiting

2,000-4,000 warm email subscribers per year not captured from podcast-specific CTAs. Conservative 3% email-to-cohort conversion: 60-120 cohort applications per year from the email list alone. That compounds: $60-120K/yr incremental from capture alone, growing with every episode published.

6 months from now ·25+ more episodes published without an outro CTA. 1,000-2,000 warm leads permanently routed back to Spotify. The list you should already have keeps not getting built.

Fix · Couples Debt Payoff Timeline Calculator quiz (Typeform, 90 seconds). Linked in every episode description. Mentioned in every outro. Captures email, feeds 8-touch nurture sequence, routes warm leads toward cohort application. $60-120K Year-1 lift from captured leads alone.
Compound Pickup
03

No Alumni Path Post-Cohort: The Relationship Ends at Week 12

Once the cohort exists, couples who complete 12 weeks and pay off meaningful debt become the highest-value people in the Rich by Intention ecosystem. They trust Anjie and RJ completely. They have results. They are the best referral source for the next cohort and the easiest buyers for any future offer. Without a structured alumni container, that relationship value is surrendered the moment the final call ends. Every successful cohort graduate who falls out of the ecosystem is a referral, a testimonial, and a recurring revenue line that did not compound.

Cost of Waiting

At 30% alumni conversion to a $97/mo Alumni Circle: 6-12 members from the first cohort alone. At 60 members across two cohort cycles: $5,820/mo / $69,840/yr recurring. This is passive relative to cohort delivery and compounds with every cohort added.

6 months from now ·Two cohorts of graduates with no alumni home. Referrals not activated. Testimonials not documented. Recurring revenue floor not built.

Fix · Rich by Intention Alumni Circle. $97/mo or $797/yr. Monthly group call. Private Skool community. Accountability pods continue. First access to future offers. Year-1 floor at 60 members: $69,840 recurring. Compounds every quarter.
